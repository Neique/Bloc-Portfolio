A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Hello everyone. My name is Winer Raymond. I am a UX Designer and artist living in the Boston Area who is eager to learn and connect the world through great user experiences and design. I strongly believe in solving problems when most people run away from them. That to me is how someone gets a lot stronger. Not only can that concept be applied to life but design as well. That is why I am so passionate about UX Design. Nothing is more satisfying to me than to make people come together and be happy through good, clean, and simple User Design Experiences!

I am a Brooklyn New Yorker who migrated to Boston when I was young and I’ve been here ever since. Usually when I’m not designing I can be found cooking, drawing, or going for long relaxing strolls around Horseneck Beach.
