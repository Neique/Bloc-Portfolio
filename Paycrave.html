Paycrave

Paycrave is a mobile food truck app that helps hungry users through convenience of food coming to them. Kind of like Uber for food. Recommendations are drawn from local food publications and experts rather than user reviews. In this project, the user would find a food truck, be able to browse through the food truck’s menu, see the driver of the truck, and the reviews. The basic inspiration for this project was to make the user experience of searching for food a lot easier and a lot less frustrating to potential users.

Styling and Branding

Here, as can be clearly seen, the dominant color is red. “Red” is a great color because it stands out and is hard to ignore. It is also pyschologically proven that Mcdonald’s, one of the most successful restaurants in the world uses dominant colors in its logo to attract customers. The way that this is done is when the customer sees the color red, it for some reason triggers thoughts of food and eating.

As for the logo, the pig represents hunger. Pigs will of course eat almost anything and are gluttons as well as nature’s natural garbage disposers but that is not to say that is how the customer to Paycrave is at all. After all, insulting the customer is bad User Experience and that is something that is trying to be avoided at all costs. The pig represents something that is always hungry but well fed because of all the numerous choices available through the Paycrave app. The text choices are “Dosis Extrabold”. This text is fun yet clean, clear and big and hard for a customer to ignore. The supporting text choices are “Avenir” and “Roboto” to fill in the titles and text respectively.
