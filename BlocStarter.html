Blocstarter

Lots of crowd-sourced fundraising web applications exist, such as Kickstarter and GoFundMe. Some revolve around charities, causes, or even products. My goal for this project was that I tried to make it a hybrid between Kickstarter and GoFundMe which are the two most popular crowd funding sites today.

Styling and Branding

What I’ve decided to do was to go with a light blue for the main color of the site, followed by a darker Navy Blue as the supporting color. The reason why I chose the color blue is because Blue psychologically proven to be approved by many people. Because blue is favored by so many people, it is often viewed as a non-threatening color that can seem conservative and traditional. Also, Blue is often seen as a sign of stability and reliability. Businesses that want to project an image of security often utilize blue in their advertising and marketing efforts. These are just a couple of the examples of why I chose the color blue for the overall design of this website.

The logo itself was meant to represent how a crowdfunding type site functions. Usually, crowdfunding starts with an idea and an idea is symbolic of a light bulb. That explains why the light bulb has been placed in the logo. The text itself that is used in the logo is “Arista Light”. I chose this font because it is rounded and friendly and inviting. Just what you need for a crowdfunding site. “Open Sans” and “Helvetica” round out the rest of the text selections to fill up the website respectively.
